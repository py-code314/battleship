Battleship

Rules

  1. Each player places their ships (The AI places its ships randomly, and it cannot see where you've placed your ships).
  2. Ships may not overlap or touch each other. Also they can only be placed within the grid boundaries.
  3. Ships can be placed either horizontally or vertically, but never diagonally.
  4. The players take turns taking shots at squares. The results are either "hit" or "miss".
  5. The winner is the first player to sink all of the other player's ships.

Instructions

  1. On initial page load, ships are placed randomly on your board.
  2. You can click the "Randomize" button to shuffle the ships' positions or drag and drop them to place them where you want.
  3. You can drag any square of a ship, and the ship will be positioned starting from the square where you drop it.
  4. You can also change a ship's direction by clicking any of its squaresâ€”the ship will be repositioned to start from the square where you clicked.
  5. Click "Play" button to start the game.
  6. You can reset the game during the play or after the game is over by using "Reset" button.
  7. On your turn, click a square on enemy board and see what happens. Square turns "red" if you hit a ship, "orange" for a missed hit.
  8. Watch "Messages" box below the boards to see game updates.
